# Learning Method

### follow method outlined in touchdesigner’s Learning Tips

- https://learn.derivative.ca/learning-resources/learning-tips/
    1. watch entire video uninterrupted (first view)
    2. take notes on things that are **interesting** and things i want to **experiment** with
    3. open touchdesigner and follow along with tutorial (second view)
    4. experiment with what i just learned + take additional notes
    5. break then next video + topic
